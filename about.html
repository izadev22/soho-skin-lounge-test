About me

My name is Tallya and I was born and raised in Israel and moved to Canada in 2007. I am a Certified Medical Laser and Medical Aesthetician with 20+ years of experience in this field. My areas of expertise are Skin Analysis, Skin Disorders, customized facials for individual skin needs, laser hair removal and IPL .

Upon my arrival in Canada I worked at a Medical Clinic in Vancouver for 5 years. After the birth of my second child I decided to return to my passion, the world of aesthetics, and pursued a career with Med Spa in Richmond, B.C., for 7 years.

I am not only entrepreneur I am a mother of 2 beautiful children Aliya (12 ) and Yehonatan (7).

My mission is to exceed the expectations of every client that walks through the door, by providing excellent service, results and care using medical grade Health Canada approved equipment operated by a professionally trained Laser Technician and skin care specialists is my guarantee.
